Partners

#EUvsVirus is a public expression of EU solidarity and support offered on a voluntary basis. Our partners and networks have pledged* to provide essential resources, mentors, know-how and perks to support this hackathon and its top ranked teams. They play a critical role in the whole hackathon process and we would like to thank them for their contribution so that we can overcome the challenges of the COVID-19 crisis together. More details about the awards and prizes provided by some partners will follow during the days of the hackathon.
